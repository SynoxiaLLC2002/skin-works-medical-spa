Services Offered by SkinWorksMed

One of the most compelling reasons people turn to SkinWorksMed is the variety and depth of its services. Unlike basic salons or skincare centers, SkinWorksMed combines medical knowledge with aesthetic artistry.

1. Facial Aesthetic Treatments

  • Medical-Grade Facials: Far more effective than your average spa facial, tailored to your skin type with pharmaceutical-grade ingredients.
  • Microneedling: Also known as collagen induction therapy, helps reduce scars, fine lines, and uneven texture.
  • Hydrafacial: A multi-step treatment that deeply cleanses, exfoliates, hydrates, and infuses the skin with antioxidants and peptides.

2. Injectables

  • Botulinum Toxin (Botox/Dysport): Relaxes facial muscles to smooth wrinkles like crow’s feet and forehead lines.
  • Dermal Fillers: Adds volume to cheeks, lips, and under-eyes, and contours facial features.
  • Lip Enhancements: Fuller, more symmetrical lips with precision injections that avoid the overdone look.

3. Laser and Light Treatments

  • Laser Hair Removal: Permanently reduces unwanted hair using safe laser light.
  • IPL Photofacial: Reduces redness, sunspots, and uneven pigmentation.
  • Skin Resurfacing: Stimulates collagen and renews skin layers to reduce wrinkles and scarring.

4. Body Contouring and Sculpting

  • Non-Surgical Fat Reduction: Cryolipolysis (fat freezing) for eliminating fat cells in targeted areas.
  • Radiofrequency Skin Tightening: Promotes collagen for firmer, lifted skin.
  • Cellulite Reduction: Smooths dimpled skin and enhances texture.

5. Acne and Scar Treatments

  • Chemical Peels: Exfoliates and reduces acne and scarring.
  • Blue Light Therapy: Targets acne-causing bacteria and calms inflammation.
  • Scar Revision: Minimizes deep scars using laser, microneedling, or fillers.

6. Anti-Aging and Rejuvenation Programs

  • Wrinkle Reduction: Combines injectables and lasers to target fine lines and volume loss.
  • Skin Tightening: Uses radiofrequency or ultrasound to lift sagging skin.
  • Custom Skincare Plans: Medical-grade products tailored to your needs for long-lasting glow.
