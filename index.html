Services Offered by SkinWorksMed

One of the most compelling reasons people turn to SkinWorksMed is the variety and depth of its services. Unlike basic salons or skincare centers, SkinWorksMed combines medical knowledge with aesthetic artistry.

1. Facial Aesthetic Treatments

  • Medical-Grade Facials: These are far more effective than your average spa facial. Tailored to your skin type, they often include exfoliation, deep cleansing, extraction, and hydration using pharmaceutical-grade ingredients.
  • Microneedling: Also known as collagen induction therapy, this procedure stimulates the skin’s natural healing process, helping to reduce scars, fine lines, and uneven texture.
  • Hydrafacial: A multi-step facial that cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates the skin while infusing antioxidants and peptides for a radiant glow.

2. Injectables

  • Botulinum Toxin (Botox/Dysport): Used to relax facial muscles and smooth out dynamic wrinkles like crow’s feet, frown lines, and forehead creases.
  • Dermal Fillers: Ideal for adding volume to areas like the cheeks, lips, and under-eyes, as well as for contouring the jawline and smoothing nasolabial folds.
  • Lip Enhancements: Precise injections create fuller, more symmetrical lips without the risk of looking overdone.

3. Laser and Light Treatments

  • Laser Hair Removal: Permanently reduce unwanted hair on the face, legs, arms, and bikini area using laser light that safely destroys the hair follicle.
  • IPL Photofacial: A treatment that uses intense pulsed light to reduce redness, sunspots, and uneven pigmentation.
  • Skin Resurfacing: Fractional laser treatments that target fine lines, wrinkles, and scarring by stimulating collagen and renewing the top layers of skin.

4. Body Contouring and Sculpting

  • Non-Surgical Fat Reduction: Techniques like cryolipolysis (fat freezing) help to eliminate fat cells in areas like the abdomen, thighs, and flanks.
  • Radiofrequency Skin Tightening: Non-invasive treatments that heat deeper skin layers to promote collagen production, resulting in firmer, more lifted skin.
  • Cellulite Reduction: Specialized therapies smooth out dimpled skin and improve overall skin texture.

5. Acne and Scar Treatments

  • Chemical Peels: These exfoliate the skin and speed up cell turnover to reduce acne breakouts and fade scars over time.
  • Blue Light Therapy: A treatment that targets acne-causing bacteria and reduces inflammation without harsh medications.
  • Scar Revision: This may include microneedling, laser therapy, or filler injections to minimize the appearance of deep scars.

6. Anti-Aging and Rejuvenation Programs

  • Wrinkle Reduction: Combining neuromodulators, fillers, and lasers to target fine lines and restore volume.
  • Skin Tightening: Non-surgical facelifts using radiofrequency or ultrasound technology to lift sagging skin.
  • Custom Skincare Plans: Medical-grade products are prescribed based on individual skin needs, helping clients maintain youthful skin long after their treatment ends.
